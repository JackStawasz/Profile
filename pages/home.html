Current Research

Calculating the Weinberg angle from neutrino interaction events. There is much literature on charged current interactions, but neutral current interactions are less studied. Using data from the NOvA experiment at Fermilab, I am working to identify neutral current events and calculate the Weinberg angle using Python and ROOT.
